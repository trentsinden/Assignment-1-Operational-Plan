Hi my name is Trent Sinden, IT Call Center Supervisor for the Town of Oakville. By implementing industry standards such as ITIL and HDI the Town of Oakville has never been in a better position to help its residents and employees. This operations plan will outline how we can implement these best practices and some of the goals we plan to reach as an organization this fiscal year.


As an organization that is constantly growing and evolving, it's important to establish goals for the upcoming fiscal year. This year we will be looking to:


  1. Standardize technology to reduce cost
  2. Update or replace outdated technology at Town Hall and Central Ops
  3. Establish distinct guidelines for work between departments
  4. Organize technology to reduce waste and improve update times

ITIL

ITIL is an IT service management framework that:

  • Increase effective communication with customers
  • Create a foundation for the IT infrastructure
  • Detail optimal ways for addressing services
  • Assist in establishing cost-effective plans when purchasing resources

HDI

HDI is a standard for support centers that:

  • Effectively establish the optimal allocation of resources
  • Advocates teamwork throughout the organization
  • Promotes the support center in the organization
  • Develop and maintain an operational plan
